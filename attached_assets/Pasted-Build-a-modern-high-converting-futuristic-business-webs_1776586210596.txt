- Dark SaaS style (similar to Stripe / Vanta / modern AI startups)

- Primary color: electric blue (#2563EB)
- Accent color: lighter blue glow (#3B82F6)
- Subtle gradients and glow effects for a futuristic feel
- Clean, minimal, grid-based layout
- Smooth animations (hover effects, fade-in, subtle motion)

- Strong headline:
  "We Build Digital Systems That Drive Growth"

- Have an icon
- Be clickable (can link to placeholder pages)
- Have hover effects (glow / lift)

Section explaining:
- Transparent communication
- No hidden costs
- Fast delivery
- Focus on real results (clients & growth)

Use bullet points + short strong statements.

4. PROCESS SECTION:

Must include:
