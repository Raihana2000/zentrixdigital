Build a premium, high-converting, futuristic website for a digital agency called "Zentrix Solutions".

GOAL:
The website must position Zentrix Solutions as a professional, trustworthy, and results-driven company that builds websites, develops custom applications, and provides ongoing maintenance and optimization. It should clearly communicate transparency, quality, and business growth.

The design must feel like a high-end SaaS/tech company (similar to Stripe, Vanta, modern AI companies).

---

DESIGN STYLE:
- Dark futuristic SaaS design
- Background: deep black (#0B0B0B)
- Text: white (#FFFFFF)
- Primary color: electric blue (#2563EB)
- Accent: soft glowing blue (#3B82F6)
- Subtle gradients and glow effects
- Clean grid layout
- Smooth animations (hover, fade-in, transitions)
- Premium and minimal (no clutter)

---

STRUCTURE:

1. HERO SECTION
- Headline:
  "We Build Digital Systems That Drive Growth"
- Subheadline:
  "Websites, applications, and scalable solutions designed to attract customers and grow your business."
- CTA buttons:
  - "Get a Free Website Check"
  - "Start Your Project"
- Background with modern abstract tech visuals or glow

---

2. ABOUT US (OVER ONS)
Create a strong section explaining:
- Who Zentrix Solutions is
- That the company focuses on building modern digital systems
- That transparency, speed, and results are core values

Example tone:
"We are a digital agency focused on building powerful websites and applications that help businesses grow. At Zentrix Solutions, we believe in transparency, clear communication, and delivering real results."

Include:
- short paragraphs
- 3–4 core values:
  - Transparency
  - Performance
  - Innovation
  - Reliability

---

3. WHAT WE DO (WAT WIJ DOEN)
Clear explanation of services:

- Website Development
  → "Modern, fast, and conversion-focused websites"

- Application Development
  → "Custom-built applications tailored to your business"

- Maintenance & Optimization
  → "Continuous improvement, updates, and performance optimization"

- Digital Growth Support
  → "Helping businesses attract more customers and scale"

Make this section visually structured and easy to scan.

---

4. WHO WE HELP (WIE WIJ HELPEN)
Create a section targeting:

- Small businesses
- Local shops (barbers, salons, garages)
- Entrepreneurs and startups
- Growing companies that want more customers

Use short descriptions per category.

---

5. SERVICES (CLICKABLE TILES)
Create modern clickable tiles/cards with hover effects:

- Websites
- Applications
- Optimization
- Growth

Each tile must:
- Have an icon
- Have hover animation (lift/glow)
- Be clickable (link to placeholder pages)
- Include short descriptions

---

6. WHY ZENTRIX (TRUST & TRANSPARENCY)
Explain:

- No hidden costs
- Clear communication
- Fast delivery
- Focus on results

Use strong bullet points.

---

7. PROCESS SECTION
Show 4 steps visually:

1. Analysis
2. Strategy
3. Development
4. Launch & Optimization

Use icons or timeline design.

---

8. REVIEWS SECTION
- Title: "What Our Clients Say"
- Create 3–4 review cards (placeholders)
- Include:
  - Name
  - Company
  - Stars
- Add text:
  "More reviews coming soon"
- Must be clean and expandable

---

9. FINAL CTA
- Headline:
  "Ready to Grow Your Business?"
- Button:
  "Contact Us Now"

10. FOOTER
Must include:

- Zentrix Solutions

- Clean, minimal layout

- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)
